Why this exists

The Story Behind JS

⚔️ A corporate war between Netscape and Microsoft gave birth to a language nobody expected to survive.
⏱️ Built in 10 days, named for hype, and shipped before it was ready — yet it outlasted every rival.
🌐 Today it runs on every browser, every server, every device. Understanding why it exists changes how you use it.

About

What is JStory?

JStory is a free, story-driven JavaScript series for people who learn better through narrative than through documentation. Follow Arjun, a student on summer break, as he goes from zero to building real things — one chapter, one concept, one "oh, that's why" moment at a time.
